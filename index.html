What Does "Dhamma" means..

Dhamma is a guidance. The way of universe, how it works, what benefits and what aid to live there, can be recognized as Dhamma.. 2500 years ago the concepts of the reality, matter and the experience came to  this world through the Dhamma. Great philosopher, spiritual leader Sri Gauthama Buddha first came up with this vast knowledge and later some of the information were added by Buddhist monks to help understanding the  things more easily.

There are two levels of dhamma. Most basic knowledge called "Sammuthi deshana" and more advanced concepts called "Paramartha deshana". Sammuthi deshana is basically giving the guidance to go through day today life while Paramartha deshana explaining more advanced topics like reality, experience, mind, matter etc.. The whole point of Dhamma is to help everyone to free from all kinds of suffer. It shows us how humans' levels of attachments (like, love, passion, greed, dislike, hatred, anger, eager, jealousy etc..) leads to suffering and shows us the practical methods to find a neutral place where we can find peace..
